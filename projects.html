Improve Air Pollution

Our pilot project is to build a crowdsourced PM2.5 air pollution sensor network.

PM2.5 air pollution is a growing problem that affects many parts of the world. Government around the world are asked to publish open data of PM2.5 index from their monitoring stations. The problem is that government and companies can only set up limited number of stations. As a result, people can not obtain meaningful insights from the data and take actions accordingly.
